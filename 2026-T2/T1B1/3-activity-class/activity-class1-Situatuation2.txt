Situation 2 – Cleaning Company

Team Leader: Manuel Perez – Cleaner Manager
Team Member: Luiz Ricardo Martins Lopes Filho – Cleaner

1. What’s the problem in your team right now?
The problem is that Luiz has been changing the cleaning schedule without telling the other cleaners or Manuel.

2. How is this problem affecting your work or the group?
It creates confusion because some jobs are left without enough workers, while other jobs have too many people. The team wastes time and sometimes arrives late to the next site.

3. What actually happened?
Last weekend, Luiz decided to go directly to a house-cleaning job instead of helping first at a large event venue. He thought it would save time, but nobody knew about the change. Because of this, the event venue was short one cleaner for almost an hour.

4. How can the leader respond in a calm and respectful way?
Manuel can speak calmly to Luiz and explain that he understands Luiz was trying to help, but changing the schedule without telling anyone creates problems for the team.

5. What solution can you both agree on together?
They can agree that if Luiz thinks the schedule should change, he must contact Manuel first before making any decision.

6. Who will do what from now on?
* Luiz will follow the schedule and ask Manuel before changing anything.
* Manuel will send the daily schedule more clearly and stay available by phone during the day.

7. How will you make sure this doesn’t happen again?
They will use a group chat for all schedule changes so every team member knows where they need to be and when.
