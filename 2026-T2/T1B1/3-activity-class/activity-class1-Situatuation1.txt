Situation 1 – Catering Company in Melbourne

Team Leader: Luiz Ricardo Martins Lopes Filho – Warehouse Manager
Team Member: Manuel Perez – Van Driver and Warehouse Assistant

1. What’s the problem in your team right now?
The problem is that Manuel has not been reporting damaged or missing stock when he returns from deliveries.

2. How is this problem affecting your work or the group?
It causes problems in the warehouse because the team only discovers the missing or damaged items when they are needed for the next job. This creates stress, delays, and sometimes the company needs to buy or replace items at the last minute.

3. What actually happened?
Last week, after a catering event, Manuel returned to the warehouse and put everything away without saying that two serving trays were broken and one drink container was missing. The next morning, another team needed those items for a delivery and realised they were not there.

4. How can the leader respond in a calm and respectful way?
Luiz can speak with Manuel privately and calmly. He can explain that mistakes happen, but it is important to report any damaged or missing items straight away so the team can fix the problem early.

5. What solution can you both agree on together?
They can agree that after every delivery, Manuel will check the stock before leaving the warehouse and report any missing or damaged items to Luiz.

6. Who will do what from now on?
* Manuel will complete a stock check sheet after every delivery and tell Luiz about any problems.
* Luiz will create a simple form for reporting damaged or missing items and review it at the end of each day.

7. How will you make sure this doesn’t happen again?
They will use the stock check form after every delivery and have a quick discussion at the end of the shift to make sure all equipment has been checked properly.
