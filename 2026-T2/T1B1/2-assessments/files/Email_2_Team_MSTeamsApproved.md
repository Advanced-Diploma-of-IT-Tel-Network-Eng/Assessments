Dear Team,

I am writing to share an important update following my recent meeting with our CEO, Lucas Lopez, regarding the team's performance and the progress of the Cloud-First SaaS & B2C project.

## CEO Decision — Microsoft Teams Approved

Following my report to Lucas outlining the team's current challenges and the solutions we have implemented together, I am pleased to inform you that the CEO has formally **approved the adoption of Microsoft Teams** as our official collaboration platform, effective **1 July 2021.**

This decision directly responds to the issues we identified together regarding information sharing and work duplication, and reflects Lucas's confidence in our team's ability to address challenges proactively and transparently.

## When Will This Change Occur?

The transition to Microsoft Teams will be implemented in two phases:

- **Phase 1 (1 July 2021):** All team members will receive access to Microsoft Teams. Hal will lead a brief onboarding session to ensure everyone is comfortable with the platform's core features.
- **Phase 2 (15 July 2021):** Microsoft Teams will become the primary communication and collaboration platform for all project-related discussions, file sharing, and meeting coordination. Trello will continue to be used for sprint and task management.

## How Will This Affect the Team?

This change will positively impact the team in the following ways:

**Reduced Work Duplication**
All project files, updates, and decisions will be centralised in Microsoft Teams, giving every team member real-time visibility of progress across all workstreams.

**Improved Remote Collaboration**
For those working remotely, Microsoft Teams will provide a single, reliable platform for video meetings, instant messaging, and document collaboration — replacing the current fragmented approach.

**Stronger Team Cohesion**
Having a dedicated team space will make it easier to maintain open communication, share knowledge, and support each other — particularly as we move into the more intensive phases of the SaaS platform development and testing.

## What Do You Need to Do?

1. Check your email for your **Microsoft Teams invitation** by **28 June 2021.**
2. Attend the **onboarding session** on **1 July 2021** *(calendar invite to follow).*
3. Begin using Microsoft Teams for all project communication from **15 July 2021.**

I am proud of how we have handled these challenges, and I am confident that with these new tools and processes in place, we are well positioned to deliver the Cloud-First SaaS & B2C project successfully.
